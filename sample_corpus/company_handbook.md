# Acme Services Handbook

## Support Coverage

Our standard support queue is monitored from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM Pacific time on business days.
Standard requests receive a first response within 4 business hours.
Priority requests receive a first response within 1 business hour.

## Escalation Path

The support lead is Maya Chen.
If an issue is still unresolved after the first response, the escalation path is:
1. Support Lead
2. Operations Manager
3. Company Owner

## Customer Communication

The person who owns the escalation also owns the next customer update.
Escalation updates should be sent every 2 hours until resolution.
