# Incident Playbook

## Outage Response

For a customer-facing outage, the communication owner is Maya Chen until the incident is handed to an executive.
The technical owner is the service owner for the affected system.

## Postmortems

Every customer-facing incident needs a postmortem in the knowledge base within 2 business days.
The postmortem must include timeline, root cause, customer impact, and follow-up actions.
